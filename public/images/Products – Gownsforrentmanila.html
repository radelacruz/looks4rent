About Us

It all started in 2010 when lawyer-turned-fashion-designer Ms. Dada Arquillo created a small fashion shop in Manila to veer from the stress of her legal career. She called it Damsel – the embodiment of her love for design. Her four-square-meter shop initially sold unique apparel and bags to fashion enthusiasts at bazaars.
